Who We Are

DANA Helping Hands is a network of young European volunteers working in Valencia and other territories, coordinating useful and close-to-community actions.

We are a registered NGO since 1998 and offer volunteering opportunities in local and international projects, focused on community development, environmental conservation, and social work through non-formal education.

What is DANA Helping Hands

An ambitious project, funded by the European Union, to support communities in the Valencian Community affected by the DANA of October 29th, 2024, over a period of 36 months.

DANA HH proposes an Intervention Plan with 19 volunteering projects to recover the affected areas, fostering collaboration between volunteers, organizations, and the local community.

In other words, joining forces to address urgent needs, help people regain their quality of life, and create sustainable changes in the social fabric.

Our initiative contributes to material recovery, community strengthening, and raising awareness about the consequences of natural disasters. Through synergy between local and international partners, we aim to restore hope, improve the quality of life of those affected, and establish a replicable model for future climate crises.
